It kept playing on my head
The day I moved
to somewhere foreign
and met
the people i treasured.
we were strangers
that were bound by fate,
but broken by it.
It was new to me,
the feeling of belonging
and because of that
i wanted to treasure
and protect it
from the bottom of my heart.
until it all crumbled to pieces,
little by little.
flying towards their own paths
and leaving it all behind
i guess it wasnt meant to be.
the time keeps on moving,
the film reel on repeat,
the cinema all for my own
the laughter
the fun
the emotions
the hatred
and the joy
feels just like days ago.
and here I am
reminiscing
about things that happened before today
hoping that one day
it will return
while they,
put the past behind,
and walked like nothing has happened
towards the next day,
towards their own fate.
i had a blast
from the past
i mourned for the loss
and tried to walk to where tomorrow brings
though struggling,
i am trying
we might not be like what we used to be
but i had fun
i really did
ill see you back at basecamp,
someday
when fate allows it.
